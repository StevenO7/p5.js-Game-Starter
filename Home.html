EV-oLUTION

EVolve with the times and become an Electric Vehicle owner today! 

As a new and innovative piece of technology, Electric Vehicles (EVs) have been receiving lots of media attention, like all innovations. Our team here at EV-olution hopes to help disperse some misconceptions regarding EVs and convince the everyday driver why their next car should be an EV.

EV Charge Time

A common problem many people have with EVs are how long they take to charge. While it is true that EVs do not have the ability to be fully charged in the same amount of time it takes to fill up a gas tank, there are many pros that help counter this issue. For starters, EVs can be charged almost anywhere, as long as there is electricity. This means that you can just charge it at home, rather then look for a station.

Charging Station Visibility 

As a new product that needs to be more widely adopted for society, another issue that comes up may be charging station visibility. Its almost impossible to spot a charging station on the side of the road, but luckily the car itself usually comes with an application that tells its driver where the closest charging station is. As time progresses, more and more stations will be available to the consumer.

Range Anxiety 

Yet another misconception about EVs comes from the idea of not having enough battery. The great thing about EVs in regards to this issue is how the EV tells you when it is low and its range is not as bad as people make it out to be. One full charge is generally good enough for 200miles or more than 300km which is more than suitable for most people who on average drive around 35miles per day.
